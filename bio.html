Biography

Hello! I am Alessandro Perego, Ph.D., a Senior Data Scientist at 3M specializing in polymer physics, material science, and scientific software development. With over eight years of experience, I combine computational modeling, experimental insights, and advanced data analysis to solve complex challenges in material engineering and science.

I earned my Ph.D. in Polymer Engineering from The University of Akron and a B.S. in Chemical Engineering from Brigham Young University. My academic journey equipped me with a deep understanding of the thermal, mechanical, and rheological properties of polymers, as well as the critical structure-property relationships that drive innovation in material science.

At 3M, I develop scalable, efficient scientific software using Python and its robust libraries, including Numpy, Scipy, TensorFlow, and Scikit-learn. My work integrates software version control (Git/GitHub), CI/CD pipelines, and large-scale data processing to transform complex data into actionable insights. This combination of expertise allows me to contribute to impactful, industry-driven projects that advance the frontiers of material science and engineering.
