About me

I’m a Data Scientist at 3M focused on building scientific software that accelerates innovation in materials research. With over nine years of experience in polymer physics, I specialize in understanding the thermal, mechanical, and rheological behavior of polymers and other complex fluids through both molecular simulations and experimental analysis. My work bridges materials science and software development. I design scalable, data-driven tools in Python using libraries such as NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, TensorFlow, and PyTorch, supported by modern practices in version control, CI/CD, and code documentation.
